HOW TO PLAY
Three game modes · one live index

Every Mogul contest is scored against the MoviePass Hype Index, a real-time sentiment dashboard that pulls trailer views, Reddit mentions, Google Trends, and trade publication coverage into a single 0-999 AMSI score. Hype Scores update every 15 minutes. When the scores move, your contest position moves with them.

HYPE CALL 📈 Pick Your Slate

The core Mogul game. Pick 5 movies from this weekend's theatrical slate plus 2 wildcard upcoming titles. You're scored on the collective AMSI movement of your picks over the contest window.

Scoring: Sum of (AMSI gain per pick × slot multiplier). Top slot gets 3× weighting, slot 2 gets 2×, slots 3-5 get 1×. Wildcards get 1.5× but only count if they hit positive.
  • Build your lineup before contest lock (usually 30 min before first-release showtime)
  • Watch your slate score update live as trailers drop, reviews land, and social momentum shifts
  • Top 10 cash in tournaments, top 5 in H2H, top half in 50/50
  • Entry fees from $1 to $100 — same rules across all tiers
Contest cadence · DAILY — locks at 4pm UTC each day

HYPE SURGE ⚡ Fast · Hourly

The quick-fix format. Pick one movie you think will gain the most mentions in the next 60 minutes. Mentions include Reddit posts, news headlines that mention the title, and social activity. The biggest mover wins.

Scoring: Delta in mention count between contest lock and settle. First place takes 60% of the pool, second 25%, third 15%. Ties split.
  • $1 entry, 10 spots, ~$8.50 payout on most contests
  • New slate posts every hour on the hour — enter up to 5 minutes before lock
  • Perfect if you spot an indie title trending on Reddit or a trailer dropping on X
  • Fastest cash-in-hand game in the lobby
Contest cadence · HOURLY — top of every hour, all day

THE MARQUEE ⭐ Weekend Championship

The flagship weekend tournament. Larger fields, bigger prize pools, and the full Friday-through-Sunday Hype Score window as your scoring period. Build a slate of 7 titles including at least one wildcard from an upcoming film more than 30 days out.

Scoring: Same multi-slot weighting as Hype Call but averaged across a 72-hour window instead of 24. Box office weekend delta counts double for any title in wide release. Top 15 finishers cash in $5 tournaments, top 8 in $25, top 4 in $100.
  • Opens Friday 00:00 UTC, locks Friday 20:00 UTC, settles Sunday 23:59 UTC
  • Also includes the Box Office Exacta sub-contest: pick the weekend #1 and #2 in order
  • Greyed out Monday-Thursday — you'll see the section in the lobby but can't enter
  • Highest average payouts of any recurring Mogul contest
Contest cadence · WEEKENDS ONLY — Fri 00:00 UTC through Sun 23:59 UTC
