How to Play

Mogul is a skill-based Daily Fantasy Sports platform. Every contest is player vs player — Mogul never wagers against you. We take a transparent rake (8–15% based on entry) shown on every card. All contests settle against the MoviePass Hype Index composite score.

1 · HYPE CALL

Daily. Predict whether a film's Hype Score rises or falls over 24 hours. Settles against Hype Index 24-hour movement. Skill contest based on knowledge of entertainment trends.

2 · HYPE SURGE

Hourly. Predict which film gains the most mentions in 60 minutes. Settles against real-time mention data. Skill contest based on social media knowledge.

3 · THE DRAFT

Weekly. Draft a portfolio of films, actors, and directors within a salary cap. Score is the combined Hype Index performance. Identical structure to traditional DFS fantasy sports.

4 · FRANCHISE RANK

Event-based. Predict where a new film ranks within its franchise. Settles against the composite Franchise Position Score: opening box office rank within franchise (40%), Hype Score peak (30%), audience sentiment (20%), critical consensus (10%). Ranking contest only — does not involve standalone prediction of box office revenue.

5 · CHARACTER CLASH

Head to Head. Two iconic characters compete on Hype Score momentum. Settles against 30-day Hype Index movement. Head-to-head skill competition.

6 · GENRE WARS

Weekly. Draft films within a single genre. Best combined Hype Score wins. Settles against Hype Index genre rankings. Portfolio skill contest identical to DFS structure.

7 · STAR POWER

Performance Score. Pick characters always tied to a specific film, displayed as [Actor] as [Character] in [Film]. Settles against Performance Score movement. Identical to athlete performance in traditional DFS.

8 · WEEKEND SURGE

Friday – Sunday. Pick films gaining the most Hype Score points over the weekend. Settles against Hype Index weekend movement. Short-duration skill contest.
